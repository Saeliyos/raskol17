The Schism

In Russian, "раскол" (Raskol) means schism, split, or fracture. Originating from GRU Directorate S, RASKOL 17 was formed post-2014 out of dissent. It is an unacknowledged detachment operating completely off the grid.

No paper trail. No uniforms. No oversight. The Federation denies their existence. RASKOL 17 is the authorized fracture made to protect the state from threats it cannot officially address. They leave no digital footprint. They carry no flag.
